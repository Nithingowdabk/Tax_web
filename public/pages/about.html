Our Story

A Decade of Trust & Excellence in Financial Services

Aswathy Associates was founded with a clear mission — to provide affordable, reliable, and professional financial services to businesses and individuals across Kerala. What started as a small practice has grown into a trusted firm serving 500+ clients across diverse industries.

We understand that navigating the complex landscape of GST, income tax, company law, and business registrations can be overwhelming. That's why we exist — to simplify compliance so our clients can focus on growing their businesses.

Our commitment to accuracy, timeliness, and client satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the most reliable accounting firms in the Thevalakkara region. We treat every client's business as our own.
