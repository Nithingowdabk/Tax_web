FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST Registration? +
Any business with an annual turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for services or special category states) must register for GST. Additionally, inter-state sellers, e-commerce operators, and certain specified categories need mandatory registration regardless of turnover.
What is the penalty for late GST return filing? +
Late filing of GST returns attracts a late fee of ₹50 per day (₹25 CGST + ₹25 SGST) for each day of delay. For nil returns, the late fee is ₹20 per day. Additionally, interest at 18% per annum is charged on outstanding tax. This can add up significantly, which is why timely filing is crucial.
Do I need a CA for Income Tax Filing? +
While simple salaried individuals can file their own returns, professional assistance is highly recommended for businesses, professionals, individuals with multiple income sources, capital gains, or foreign income. A CA ensures maximum deductions, proper compliance, and protection against scrutiny.
How long does Company Incorporation take? +
With all documents in place, Private Limited Company incorporation typically takes 7-15 working days. This includes DSC generation, DIN application, name approval, and SPICe+ form filing. LLP registration usually takes 5-10 working days.
Can I upload documents securely through your portal? +
Yes! Our secure client portal allows you to upload documents (PDF, JPG, PNG) with complete encryption. You can categorize documents by service type, track their review status, and receive notifications when we've processed them. No more risky email attachments.
