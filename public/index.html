❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our services

What documents are needed for GST registration? +
You'll need PAN card, Aadhaar card, proof of business registration, bank account details, business address proof, and passport-sized photos. Our team will guide you through the exact requirements based on your business type.
How long does GST registration take? +
GST registration typically takes 3-7 working days from the date of application. With our expedited service, we can often complete it within 3-4 working days.
What are the penalties for late GST filing? +
Late filing attracts ₹50/day (₹20/day for NIL returns) as late fee, plus 18% annual interest on outstanding tax. This is why we ensure all our clients file on time without fail.
Do you provide services for individual tax filing? +
Yes! We provide ITR filing services for salaried individuals, freelancers, professionals, and HUFs. Our services include tax planning, return preparation, and e-filing.
Can I track my filing status online? +
Absolutely! Through our client portal, you can track all your filings, view documents, check deadlines, and communicate directly with your assigned CA in real time.
What are your payment options? +
We accept UPI, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and online payments through our secure portal. You can also opt for monthly billing for recurring services.
